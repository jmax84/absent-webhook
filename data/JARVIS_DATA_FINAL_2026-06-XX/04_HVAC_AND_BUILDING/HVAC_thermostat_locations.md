# HVAC Thermostat Locations



This file helps JARVIS answer questions about thermostat locations in the facility.



The thermostat map is located in:



`HVAC_thermostat_locations.pdf`



## What the PDF Shows



Page 1 shows thermostat locations across the facility, including:



* Warehouse 4 / Mailshop

* Warehouse 3

* Warehouse 2 / Envelopes

* Warehouse 1

* Pre-Press

* I.T.

* Ink Department / Ink Shop

* Maintenance

* Lunch room areas

* Office areas

* Shipping and Receiving

* Envelope Parts Room

* Training Room

* Safety Office



## JARVIS Use



JARVIS may use this file when someone asks questions like:



* Where is the thermostat for the envelope department?

* Where is the thermostat for Warehouse 2?

* Where is the thermostat for Warehouse 4?

* Where is the thermostat near Pre-Press?

* Where is the thermostat near the ink room?

* Where is the thermostat near maintenance?



## Important Limitation



The map should be used as a location guide.



If someone asks whether an HVAC unit is working, cooling, frozen, off, or in alarm, JARVIS should not assume current status from this map.



For current HVAC problems, JARVIS should ask for:



* Area affected

* Current temperature

* Thermostat reading

* Whether air is blowing

* Whether the unit is cooling

* Photos or video if available



## JARVIS Answer Style



If someone asks where a thermostat is, JARVIS should answer:



"The thermostat location is shown on the thermostat map. Look near [area/location from map]. If the map is unclear, physically verify or ask a supervisor."



If JARVIS cannot clearly identify the exact thermostat from the map, it should say:



"I can see the thermostat map, but I cannot confidently identify the exact thermostat for that area. Please physically verify or ask Jonathan/supervision."



## Do Not Guess Rule



JARVIS should not guess thermostat locations if the map does not clearly answer the question.
