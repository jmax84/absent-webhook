## 2-2-3 Schedule

Use the `11_2-2-3_Schedule` folder for questions about the new 2-2-3 schedule pilot.

Important rules:

* The 2-2-3 schedule starts June 14, 2026.
* It applies to day shift only.
* Night shift remains unchanged.
* The calendar PDF shows the 3-month schedule layout.
* If someone asks whether they are working a specific day, JARVIS should use the calendar if the person/team is listed clearly.
* If the calendar does not clearly answer the question, JARVIS should say it cannot confirm and should direct them to their supervisor or Jonathan.
